Privacy Policy

The Echoes of Home (“we”, “our”). Last updated: 2026-05-06. Replace this date after substantive changes.

Summary. The Echoes of Home helps you capture family memories: recipes, stories, and voices, so you can treasure them forever. Your content is tied to your account and stored using Supabase (database and private file storage). Processing of audio and text may use OpenAI (transcription, translation, structuring, and optional imagery). Sign-in may use Google via Supabase. This page is public so you can read our practices without creating an account.

1. What we collect

  • Account information: such as name and email from your identity provider (e.g. Google) and a unique user id from Supabase Auth.
  • Voice and audio: audio you record or upload for transcription and preservation.
  • Text and structured content: transcripts, translations, recipe fields, notes, and similar outputs from our processing pipeline.
  • People profiles: names, relationships, bios, notes, and optional photos you add for narrators.
  • Technical data: standard server logs (e.g. on Railway) and limited client error reports (error message, component stack, page URL) if the app fails in a way that triggers our error reporter.

2. How we use data

  • To authenticate you and provide the service.
  • To transcribe, translate, structure, and store your memories as you request.
  • To improve reliability (e.g. diagnosing crashes from error reports).

3. Where data is processed and stored

Data is stored in Supabase (region per your project configuration). Audio and media may be stored in private storage with access controlled by your account. Some content is sent to OpenAI for processing; see OpenAI’s policies for their handling of API data. The application may be hosted on Railway or similar infrastructure.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use subprocessors (Supabase, OpenAI, Google for sign-in, hosting providers) strictly to operate the service. We do not make your recordings or memories publicly discoverable from within the product.

5. Retention and deletion

Content is kept until you delete it or delete your account. You may delete your account and associated data from the in-app Account page when signed in. Deletion behavior follows our backend implementation at the time of your request.

6. Children

The service is intended for adults and families managing their own archive. It is not directed at children under 13.

7. International users

Your data may be processed in the United States or other regions where our providers operate, depending on provider configuration.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, email theechoesofhomesupport@gmail.com or use our support page.
