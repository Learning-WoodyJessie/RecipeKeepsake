Recipe Keepsake

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026
Family use · Private by design

Recipe Keepsake is a personal app for capturing and preserving family recipes narrated by loved ones. We take your privacy seriously — your recordings and recipes belong to you and your family only.

What we collect

  • Voice recordings — audio you record in the app, stored privately in your account
  • Name and email address — provided via Google Sign-In for account creation
  • Recipe data — dish names, ingredients, steps, and notes generated from your recordings
  • Device information — basic app usage data for debugging (no advertising identifiers)

How we use your data

  • Voice recordings are sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription
  • Transcriptions are processed by OpenAI GPT-4 to extract structured recipe information
  • A representative dish image may be generated using OpenAI DALL-E 3
  • All processed data is stored in your private account — we never share it with third parties
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Data storage

  • Recipe data and account information is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Audio recordings are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket — not publicly accessible
  • Recipe images are stored in a public Supabase Storage bucket (accessible only via your recipe's share link)
  • Data is hosted in the United States

Data sharing

We share data only with the following service providers, solely to operate the app:

  • OpenAI — audio transcription, recipe structuring, image generation
  • Supabase — database and file storage
  • Google — authentication (Sign in with Google)
  • Railway — application hosting

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Your rights

  • Access — you can view all your recipes within the app at any time
  • Deletion — contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export — contact us to request a copy of your data

Children's privacy

Recipe Keepsake is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
